Predicting The Energy Output Of Wind Turbine Based On Weather Condition Using Machine Learning

Wind energy plays an increasing role in the supply of energy world-wide. The energy output of a wind farm is highly dependent on the wind conditions present at its site. If the output can be predicted more accurately, energy suppliers can coordinate the collaborative production of different energy sources more efficiently to avoid costly overproduction.

So, We have developed a Machine Learning Model which can be used to predict OUTPUT POWER of ‘The Wind Power-Plant’ depending on whether conditions for a future time.

How The Predicting Model Works ?

1. Set the Time Range for Prediction (Upto 72 hours)

2. Enter Redius of Turbine Rotor

3. Enter Efficiency of Turbine

Done !!!
Then, Our model will suggest you the best time from current time to next 72 hours ( or in the selected time range ) to utilize the energy from wind farm. So that the turbine returns maximum OUTPUT POWER .

NOTE : This model is trained on Wind Turbine Scada Dataset on 2018 of a Wind Turbine in Turkey.
